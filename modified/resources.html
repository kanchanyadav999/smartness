Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from our learners

Do I need a tech background to join? ▾

Not at all! Our courses start from absolute basics. Many of our successful students come from non-tech backgrounds like commerce, arts, and science.

How long are the courses? ▾

Courses range from 4 to 9 months depending on the path. Data Analytics is 4-6 months, Data Science is 6-9 months, and DevOps is 5-7 months.

Are the classes live or recorded? ▾

All classes are live with industry experts. You also get lifetime access to recordings so you can revise anytime.

What if I miss a class? ▾

No worries — all sessions are recorded. You can watch the recording and ask doubts in the next live session or through our community support.

Is there a refund policy? ▾

Yes, we offer a 7-day no-questions-asked refund policy if you're not satisfied with the course.

Do you provide certificates? ▾

Yes! You receive a verified completion certificate that you can share on LinkedIn and add to your resume.
